Social Media Consulting: Mi Padrino

In Winter 2017, I enrolled in a project based course, SI 335: Social Media in Organizations where I was able to work with the Founder and CEO of Mi Padrino, a local Ann Arbor start up in working with their social media platform. Mi Padrino is a event planning website that is tailored towards the Hispanic/Latino demographics where it acts as an event registry and also a payment collector for gifts. My group and I advised Kim and her company to attract new customers to her platform through social media platforms such as Facebook and Twiter. Mi Padrino just released its beta version in April 2017 and expects a full launch with investor backing in September 2017. Check out Mi Padrino's website here!
